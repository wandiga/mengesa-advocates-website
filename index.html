About us

Mengesa Advocates was founded in Nairobi, Kenya. The founder, Sheillah Mengesa, saw a need to develop a leading legal firm with diversity, accessibility and affordability at it's core. By diligently and successfully serving clients across the East African region, the small but diligent team now has over 800 cases under its belt.
